Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Bestie Collabs ("we," "our," "us") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, username, login credentials.
  • Profile and content data: information you upload or share within the platform.
  • Usage data: device details, IP address, browser, and activity logs.
  • Payment data: billing details, payout information, and related transaction records.

3. How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Facilitate collaboration between users.
  • Process payments and provide support.
  • Detect fraud, enforce Terms, and ensure safety.
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, or policies.

4. Sharing of Information

  • Service providers and vendors who support our operations.
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect rights.
  • Successors in case of a business transfer (e.g., merger or acquisition).
  • We do not sell your personal data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your account information.
  • Request correction or removal of personal data.
  • Opt out of non-essential emails or marketing.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed to provide the Service, fulfill obligations, or comply with legal requirements. Afterward, we delete or anonymize data.

7. Security

We use encryption, access controls, and other safeguards to protect data. No system is perfectly secure, but we take steps to minimize risk.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from minors below the minimum age of consent in their jurisdiction. If discovered, such data will be deleted.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be posted, and continued use means you accept the changes.
