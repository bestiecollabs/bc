DMCA Policy

We respect intellectual property rights and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

If you believe your copyrighted work has been used on our site without authorization, please send a written notice that includes:

  • Your contact information
  • A description of the work you claim is infringed
  • The URL or location of the material
  • A statement that you believe the use is unauthorized
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the information is accurate
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Send all notices to: collabsbestie@gmail.com

We will review and respond in line with DMCA requirements.
