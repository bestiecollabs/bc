About Bestie Collabs

Simple tools that reward real results.

Our mission

We help Brands and Creators find their best collabs. Brands should only work with Creators who can sell. Creators should never pay for samples.

What we believe

  • Pay for results, not uncertainty.
  • Collabs should be mutually beneficial.
  • This is the fairest way to collab.

FAQ

  • How it works: Brands invite Creators and ship free samples. Creators post and earn from sales.
  • Brand fees: 10% commission per sale from collabs, plus the agreed creator commission.
  • Creator payouts: Automatic on the 1st and 15th each month.
  • Express payout: $15 fee, $50 minimum balance to withdraw.
  • Samples: Brands must provide free samples and free shipping.
  • Posting: Creators can post as much as they want. No negative talk about the brand in posts.
  • Issues: If you dislike a product or it's defective, use the built in chat to resolve it with the brand.
  • Timeline: Creators have 7 days to post. Extensions require brand approval.
  • Ad boosting: Coming soon.
  • Payments: Bestie manages relationships and payouts so creators get paid.
