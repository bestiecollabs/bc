How we use cookies and how you can control them.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies to improve your experience on our site. Cookies help us remember your preferences, analyze traffic, track sales, and provide relevant content.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you block or delete cookies, but some features of our site may not work properly without them.

We may also use third-party cookies from analytics providers or advertising partners. These cookies help us measure site performance, deliver more relevant ads, and attribute sales to campaigns or partners.

How to Manage Cookies

  • Check your browser settings to allow, block, or delete cookies.
  • Most browsers let you set preferences for all sites or specific sites.
  • You can also use “private” or “incognito” mode to limit cookie storage.

By using our site, you agree to our use of cookies.
